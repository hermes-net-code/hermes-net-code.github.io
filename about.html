Waypoint Technology

Waypoint technology allows drones to fly autanomously in a set pattern. this will allow hermes net to automatically fly in a way that provide optimal coverage over the affected area

Runway Independant

Hermesnet will be runway independant, meaning it can be launched from many different locations and does not require a large area for take off.







Cellular Repeater Technology

Cellular repeaters abord our drones will amplify the signal from far away cell towers delivering service to the affected area.
