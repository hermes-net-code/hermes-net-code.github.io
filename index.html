ABOUT

TEAM

Hermes Net is an autonomous drone system that utilizes waypoint technology and cellular repeaters to rapidly deploy cellular service over an area affected by a disaster.













Vision

To provide relief to people affected by disasters through high-quality cellular service via a network of autonomous aircraft.









Mission

To be the lifeline for communication after a disaster. At Hermes Net, our mission is to restore rapid and reliable relief in the aftermath of a natural disaster.
